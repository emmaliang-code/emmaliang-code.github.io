About

I am a recent graduate from Dalhousie University, where I earned a Bachelor of Community Design + Planning with a double major in Urban Planning & Design and Sustainability. Originally from Vancouver, BC, I've been inspired by the region's ability to balance urban vitality with natural landscapes. My work is guided by a commitment to integrating social equity and environmental responsibility into comprehensive planning frameworks that shape more resilient and inclusive communities.

Professionally, I've gained experience across both the public and non-profit sectors, contributing to multidisciplinary project management teams and supporting large-scale development projects through various master planning phases. I'm passionate about bridging design thinking, policy, and community engagement in ways that foster connection and well-being.
