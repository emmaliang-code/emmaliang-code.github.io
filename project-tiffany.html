DMA Study Coburg, AUS

Concept Plan · March 2025

Project Details

Location: Coburg, Melbourne, Vic, Australia

Type: Function of Uses Assessment

Duration: 3 months

Application: QGIS

Project Overview

This Density Mix Accessibility (DMA) study examines the urban growth potential and planning considerations for the Coburg district in Melbourne, Australia. The project analyzes existing infrastructure, community needs, and sustainable development opportunities to inform future planning decisions.

Key Objectives

  • Assess current land use patterns and zoning regulations
  • Identify opportunities for increased density while maintaining neighborhood character
  • Evaluate transportation infrastructure and connectivity needs
  • Develop recommendations for sustainable urban development
  • Engage with community stakeholders to understand local priorities

Outcomes

The study provided comprehensive recommendations for balanced urban growth that respects the area's heritage while accommodating future population increases. Key findings included strategies for transit-oriented development, green space preservation, and mixed-use zoning to create a more vibrant, walkable community.
